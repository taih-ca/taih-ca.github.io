About

An inclusive home for AI at UCalgary

The Transdisciplinary Artificial Intelligence Hub (TAIH) is a collaborative research initiative dedicated to advancing interdisciplinary AI to address complex societal and technological challenges. We unite researchers, students, and industry partners to drive high-impact innovation.

The Hub fosters an inclusive and collaborative space where members from all faculties can contribute, connect, and facilitate AI research — bringing together experts in engineering, medicine, and the social implications of AI to create inclusive and implementable work.
